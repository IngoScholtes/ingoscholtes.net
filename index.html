      Welcome

      I am an SNSF-Professor for Data Analytics in the Department of Informatics at University of Zurich. My research focuses on problems at the intersection between data analytics, network science, and machine learning.

      In 2014 I received a Juniorfellowship from the German Informatics Society (GI e.V.). In 2018 I was awarded an SNSF Professorship from the Swiss National Science Foundation.
